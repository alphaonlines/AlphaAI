Signals to watch

  • Latency/cost vs. quality for GPT-4o, Claude 3, Gemini, and open-weight gateways.
  • GPU lead times, regional data-center expansions, and interconnect availability.

Infrastructure stack

Compute & cloud

NVIDIA H100/Blackwell remain the standard; AMD MI300 and Intel Gaudi press on total cost. Hyperscalers layer custom silicon (TPU v5, Trainium, Inferentia, Maia) with premium model endpoints.NVIDIAGoogle

Networking, storage, power

InfiniBand/Ethernet fabrics (NVIDIA, Arista, Broadcom, Cisco) and fast NVMe storage (NetApp, Pure, VAST) keep clusters fed. Power availability is the gating factor as IEA projects ~2x demand by 2030.IEA

Edge & on-prem

Enterprises pursue latency control with Jetson/Qualcomm edge, Apple devices, and on-prem clusters (Dell/HPE/Supermicro) powered by open weights and gateways.NVIDIA

Risks

  • Supply chain fragility for advanced packaging and interconnects.
  • Energy constraints and community pushback on new data-center builds.
  • Latency/cost creep from network bottlenecks and data egress.
