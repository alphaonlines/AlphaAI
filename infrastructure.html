Executive snapshot

Capacity Networking Robotics

Quick summary

  • GPU supply (H100/Blackwell, MI300) and interconnect density are pacing AI capacity.
  • Hyperscalers differentiate through model access (OpenAI, Gemini, Anthropic) and data services.
  • Robotics/autonomy rides the same compute wave, with simulation and safety stacks as gatekeepers.
  • Edge/on-prem builds rise as enterprises seek latency control and data-locality guarantees.
  • Watch power availability: U.S. data center demand could double by 2030.

Who leads and why

NVIDIA

Dominant in training GPUs and networking (InfiniBand); CUDA ecosystem is a moat.

AMD & Intel

Challengers with MI300 and Gaudi lines; competitive on price/performance where supply is tight.

Hyperscalers

AWS, Azure, Google pair custom silicon (Trainium, Maia, TPU) with top model access and global regions.

Robotics builders

ABB, Fanuc, Boston Dynamics, NVIDIA Isaac + ROS 2 enable simulation-first deployments with safety layers.
