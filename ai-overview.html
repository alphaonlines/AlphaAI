Quick summary

  • Top labs: OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, Meta, Mistral, Cohere; momentum driven by multimodal quality and latency/cost.
  • Enterprise reach hinges on trust, uptime, and policy tooling as much as raw benchmarks.
  • Open-weight options (Llama, Mixtral) broaden on-prem and edge adoption; gateways normalize APIs.
  • Capital is flowing to GPU capacity and in-house accelerators; supply remains a gating factor.
  • Track funding cadence, regional data center build-outs, and policy shifts to gauge durable share.

Who leads and why

OpenAI

Fastest product velocity; deep Microsoft tie-in; GPT-4o quality and early agent ecosystem.

Google

Gemini and search integration; strong multi-modal research bench; global distribution via Workspace/Cloud.

Anthropic

Safety-forward positioning and reliable latency attract regulated industries; anchored by Amazon/Google cloud deals.

Meta & open weights

Llama 3 and community fine-tunes keep open-weight momentum high; ideal for on-prem and edge control.
