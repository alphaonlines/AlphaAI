5-minute AI timeline

1950s–2010s

Early AI, expert systems, and deep learning breakthroughs; ImageNet (2012) ignites modern neural nets.

2017

Transformers introduced; sequence models scale and enable foundation models.

2022–2023

ChatGPT and GPT-4 popularize generative AI; open-weight models (LLaMA) accelerate community adoption.

2024

GPU supply shocks; hyperscalers announce AI superclusters; Claude and Gemini join the front-runners.

2025

Multi-modal agents, robotics pilots, and on-prem open weights shape hybrid deployment playbooks.

What happened in the last 12 months

Headlines

  • Foundation model vendors pushed multimodal agents into production pilots.
  • Hyperscalers expanded GPU/TPU capacity; alternative accelerators gained share.
  • Robotics/automation trials accelerated in warehouses and brownfield factories.
  • Safety, IP, and data-localization policies tightened enterprise deals.

Signals to watch

  • Latency/cost vs. quality trade-offs across leading APIs.
  • GPU lead times and regional data center expansions.
  • Open-weight momentum enabling on-prem and edge deployments.
  • Funding cadence for model labs and infrastructure providers.
