Signals to monitor

Model release cadence

Open-weight suites like Llama push weekly refreshes while proprietary stacks (GPT-4.1, Gemini) emphasize safety layers. Watch for benchmarked latency and context window improvements.

AI supply chain

GPU lead times remain tight; Ethernet-first clusters and liquid cooling pilots are spreading beyond hyperscalers. Track capex statements from cloud providers for timing.

Policy & compliance

Model safety reporting, licensing for foundation models, and export controls on advanced accelerators determine how fast workloads can scale across regions.

Enterprise adoption

Proof-of-concept to production cycles are shortening—monitor vendor security reviews, data residency assurances, and fine-tuning cost curves.
